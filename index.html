Project

Role: Team Leader

Title: Sales prediction and analysis of city bread

Description: This project is used to predict the future sales of the company city bread. Here we have developed a model to analyze the sales of different products and predict the sale of product based on variable factors like holidays, seasons, ingredients cost, and social media trends. Linear regression, gradient boosting is used, and the classification is done using Random Forest Classifier. Predictive analytics uses historical data to predict future events. Typically, historical data is used to build a mathematical model that captures important trends. Data-driven predictive models can help companies solve long-standing problems in new ways. Our project will be done around City Bread sales. The dataset is collected from City Bread from the year 2014. The dataset consists of total number of products and number of products sold each day. This data is used to train and test the model. The prediction results are then compared with the real values and accuracy of the model is calculated.
